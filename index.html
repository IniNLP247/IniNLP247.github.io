About Me

My Story

My journey began with curiosity and a desire to understand the world through technology. Early in my university years, I faced uncertainty about my path, exploring different areas of Computer Science without a clear focus. That changed when I discovered Artificial Intelligence, which became the driving force behind my projects and ambitions.

Through experimentation and learning, I combined NLP, Computer Vision, and speech technologies into Kenkō, a system designed to bridge these domains. Kenkō represents my dedication to innovation, disciplined growth, and building meaningful tools that reflect both my vision and the possibilities of AI.

Background

My early years in university were a mix of exploration and uncertainty. While I was determined to succeed, I often felt unsure of what direction to take or which area of Computer Science would become my true passion. This period taught me resilience and the value of disciplined learning, even when the path wasn't clear.

The turning point came when I discovered Artificial Intelligence. I became captivated by its potential to solve complex problems, understand human behavior, and create systems that interact intelligently with the world. I began experimenting with projects in NLP and Computer Vision, gradually integrating them into a single multimodal system. These experiences shaped my technical expertise and clarified my long-term vision.

Inspired by the possibilities of AI, I created Kenkō, a project designed to combine NLP, CV, and speech technologies into a cohesive system. Kenkō is more than just a tool—it is the culmination of my passion for innovation, my drive to learn, and my commitment to building systems that are both practical and meaningful.

What Drives Me

I am motivated by challenges and the complexity of building something meaningful. Each project pushes me to learn, adapt, and grow. My goal isn't just to create technology—it's to make a difference, leave a mark, and be remembered for building tools that matter.

I thrive on solving problems that test my limits, and I am driven by the idea of turning ambitious visions into reality. Every line of code, every experiment, and every breakthrough brings me closer to shaping something lasting, impactful, and innovative.
